Scheduling a Meeting Made Easy

IPVideoTalk Meetings help organizations hold powerful remote meetings, trainings, classes and video conferences that can be attended on nearly any device. It allows users to instantly create a virtual collaboration space, automatically invites participants, provides meeting reminders and requires no extra software or plug-ins. IPVideoTalk Meetings lets you participate whether you are home, traveling or on-the-go.

Join and host IPVideoTalk Meetings from:
  • PCs and MACs (supports Google Chrome, Mozilla Firefox, Safari, and Opera)
  • Android and iOS devices (smartphones and tablets): free IPVideoTalk app
  • Grandstream's GVC3200 series SIP video conferencing device
  • VoIP phones, land-lines and cell phones (join-only)
